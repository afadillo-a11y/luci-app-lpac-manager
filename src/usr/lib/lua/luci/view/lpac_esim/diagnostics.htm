<%:Modem Reset Actions%>
<%:Soft Reset%>

<%:Radio off/on cycle. Fastest recovery without USB re-enumeration. Uses the active transport protocol automatically.%>

<%:USB Port Re-init%>

<%:Drop and re-enumerate the USB port via sysfs. Equivalent to physically unplugging and reinserting the modem.%>

<%:SIM Card Power Cycle%>

<%:Power off and back on the SIM slot. Forces the modem to re-read the eUICC card without a full reboot.%>

<%:Hard Reboot%>

<%:Full modem OS reboot with automatic fallback cascade. Use when profile switch does not take effect.%>
